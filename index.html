First Name: Lucas


The meaning of my first name Lucas is : bringer of light or light-giving. This is because Lucas means "bringer of light" in Latin. My parents choose this name for me because they called me "Little Chris(my dad's name)", and I guess it sounded like Lucas. The origin of my name is from Greek, Latin. It is pronouced LOO-kəs(English).

Last Name: Allen


In Celtic the meaning of my last name(Allen) is: Harmony, stone, or noble. It also may be derived from Celtic Aluinn, which means "Handsome". This name used to be a saint's name, but it was introduced again in Britian during the Norman Conquest. Then name remained popular in the 19th century. It is prounced AL-ən(English)
